## Notes

- Nutritional values may vary slightly depending on whether the fish is wild-caught or farm-raised
- Omega-3 content is particularly high in fatty fish such as salmon, mackerel, herring, and sardines
- White fish varieties (cod, tilapia, etc.) are typically lower in fat and calories
- Fish higher in mercury (swordfish, king mackerel, etc.) should be consumed less frequently, especially by pregnant women and young children
